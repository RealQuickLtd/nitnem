Read your daily Nitnem banis anytime, anywhere — no internet needed. All 13 banis are bundled right in the app.

Left off mid-reading? The app remembers where you were and offers to pick up where you stopped.

Includes Japji Sahib, Jaap Sahib, Chaupai Sahib, Rehras Sahib, Kirtan Sohila, Anand Sahib, Sukhmani Sahib, Shabad Hazare, both Tavprasad Savaiye, Asa Di Vaar, Aarti, and Ardas.

Features:
- Adjustable font size
- Auto-scroll with speed control
- Resume reading from where you left off
- Gurmukhi, English, and Hindi transliteration
- Sukhmani Sahib Astpadi tabs for quick navigation
- Center align option
- Fullscreen mode
- Keep screen on while reading
- Per-bani font size and scroll speed

Free, open source, no ads, no tracking. Built with a clean modern interface.

Source code: https://github.com/RealQuickLtd/nitnem
